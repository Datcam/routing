Contribute Memory
Gift
Instructions

Submit your favorite memories with the recipient (Submit 3 or more)

Suggestions

Review photos to jog your memory

Choose a specific moment rather than a period

Mention the details of the memories -- the sights, smells, and how you felt

Examples

That morning a few summers ago when we swam in the sea. I remember thinking it was the happiest time of my life.

That time you drove me to the lake because I had been sick for a week. I felt so thankful to have you as a friend.

The day I asked you to marry me, I'll never forget the look in your eyes and how I felt when you said yes.
