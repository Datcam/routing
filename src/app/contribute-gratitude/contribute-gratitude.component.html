Gift
Instructions

Write a message starting with “I’m grateful you’re in my life because...”

Suggestions

Focus on how their presence in your life has affected you - use “I” and “me” language.

Make this message about impact on you - not necessarily about them.

Examples

I’m grateful you’re in my life because you’ve helped me grow so much as a person.

I’m grateful you’re in my life because I feel myself when I’m around you.

I’m grateful you’re in my life because you remind me that life can be simple and beautiful if I just open myself up.
