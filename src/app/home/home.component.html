A Meaningful Gift

Collaborative

A Cogivi gift is made by a group of contributors that you select. Each one submits content for the recipient like favorite memories and photographs.

Beautiful

All of the contributions are infused into charming and elegant objects, which are packaged together to create a gift to last forever.

Simple

As the orginizer, you’ll invite contributors to work with. We’ll track their progress, send reminders, and let you know when the gift is ready.
