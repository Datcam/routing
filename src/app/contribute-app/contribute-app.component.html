Contribute Appreciation
Gift
Instructions

Write a message starting with: "What I appreciate about you is..."

Suggestions

Focus on qualities and behavior that you admire

Think about your favorite time spent with the recipient. What stood out to you about them in those moments?

Examples

What I appreciate about you is your resilience. Last year was such a terrible time and you were a beacon of light every day.

What I appreciate about you is your humor. I remember not to take my life too seriously whenever we're together.

What I appreciate about you is your vitality. You bring energy to whatever situation you're in and I love that about you.
